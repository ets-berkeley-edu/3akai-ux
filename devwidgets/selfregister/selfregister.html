Welcome to the CalCentral Pilot.

Your Information and Content

Your information and content will initially be shared by CalCentral as outlined below. You may change your permissions settings to adjust how this information is shared. Sharing this information will allow you to more fully collaborate with peers at UC Berkeley.

Only Viewable by UC Berkeley CalCentral Users

Can be changed in 'My Account' -> 'Privacy Settings':

  • Your CalCentral memberships (including your classes)
  • Your CalCentral contacts
  • Your CalCentral content (in "My Library")

Can be changed in 'My Profile':

  • Your UC Berkeley role
  • Your major
  • Your college

Public (This cannot be changed)

  • Your name

Private (Can be changed in 'My Profile')

  • Your email address

Limitations of CalCentral Pilot Program

  • This system is in active development. The design and features presented may change at any time.
  • As we improve CalCentral it may be necessary to delete documents, messages and/or content from the system. Please make backups of all documents and content that you create or share in CalCentral.
